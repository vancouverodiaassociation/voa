About VOA

The Vancouver Odia Association (VOA) is a not-for-profit social and cultural organization dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich cultural heritage of Odisha among the Odia community in Vancouver. Founded with the vision of building a strong and united Odia community, VOA serves as a platform to connect people from Odisha and celebrate our traditions, language, and culture.
