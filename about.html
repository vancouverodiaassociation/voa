About Vancouver Odia Cultural Association

About the Vancouver Odia Cultural Association (VOCA)

Welcome to the Vancouver Odia Cultural Association (VOCA)! We are a newly registered non-profit society, though our roots in the community run deep, tracing back to our humble beginnings in 2013. For over a decade, VOCA has been the vibrant hub for the Odia diaspora across Canada, particularly here in British Columbia.

Our Mission:

At VOCA, our core mission is simple yet profound: to unite all Odias living in Canada and passionately promote and preserve the rich tapestry of Odia culture, heritage, and traditions. We believe in fostering a strong, supportive, and extended family away from home, ensuring that the unique spirit of Odisha thrives in the Canadian landscape.

Our Vision:

We envision a thriving Odia community in Canada that is deeply connected to its cultural roots, while also contributing positively to the multicultural mosaic of this nation. We strive to be a beacon for future generations, instilling in them a sense of pride in their heritage and a strong understanding of Odia values.

What We Do:

VOCA is more than just an association; it's a living, breathing celebration of Odia identity. Throughout the year, we organize a diverse range of events that bring our community together, strengthen bonds, and offer a taste of Odisha right here in Canada. Our calendar is filled with:

Vibrant Cultural Celebrations:

We meticulously organize and celebrate major Odia and Indian festivals, ensuring that the traditions and customs are passed down and enjoyed by all. These include:

  • Holi: The festival of colors, bringing joy and camaraderie.
  • Diwali: The festival of lights, symbolizing triumph of good over evil.
  • Utkal Diwas: A significant day commemorating the formation of the state of Odisha, filled with cultural performances and reflections on our heritage.
  • Rath Yatra: The majestic chariot festival, a spiritual and communal spectacle.
  • Ganesh Pooja & Saraswati Pooja: Reverent celebrations dedicated to Lord Ganesha and Goddess Saraswati, fostering spiritual well-being and promoting education and arts.

Engaging Community Activities:

Beyond religious festivals, we believe in holistic community engagement. Our activities are designed to cater to diverse interests and age groups:

  • Sports Day: Promoting health, fitness, and friendly competition within the community.
  • Hiking & Camping: Embracing the natural beauty of Canada while fostering teamwork and outdoor adventure among our members.
  • Promoting Odia Arts & Literature: We actively encourage and showcase Odia artistic talents, including music, dance, literature, and traditional crafts, providing a platform for expression and appreciation.
  • Community Support & Networking: VOCA serves as a valuable resource for newly arrived Odias, helping them integrate into Canadian society and connecting them with established community members for support and networking opportunities.

Join us at the Vancouver Odia Cultural Association as we continue to build a vibrant and thriving community, preserving our cherished traditions and creating new memories together. We welcome all who share an interest in Odia culture to become a part of our growing family!
