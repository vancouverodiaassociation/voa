About Vancouver Odia Cultural Association

Our Mission

The Vancouver Odia Cultural Association (VOCA) is a not-for-profit social and cultural organization dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich cultural heritage of Odisha among the Odia community in Vancouver. Founded with the vision of building a strong and united Odia community, VOCA serves as a platform to connect people from Odisha and celebrate our traditions, language, and culture.

We aim to:

  • Promote Odia language, culture, and traditions in Vancouver
  • Create a platform for cultural exchange between Canada and Odisha
  • Support new immigrants from Odisha in their integration to Canadian society
  • Organize cultural events celebrating Odia festivals and traditions
  • Build a strong, supportive community for Odia people in Vancouver
